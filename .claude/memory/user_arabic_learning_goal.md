Primary goal of learning Arabic is reading across the full breadth of Arabic literature — classical poets (including Sicilian Arabic poets from ~1000 years ago), Quran + Quranic commentaries, and all the literary tradition that references the Quran. User is not Muslim but recognizes the Quran as foundational to Arabic literary culture.

**How to apply:** Classical/Quranic Arabic vocabulary is directly relevant, not a distraction from learning goals. When filtering vocabulary or making decisions about word relevance, classical Arabic words should be included rather than filtered out. The MSA/Classical divide matters less for this user than for someone learning Arabic for business or travel.
