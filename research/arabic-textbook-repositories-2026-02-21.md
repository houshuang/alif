## 1. Verified Free Downloads (Best Sources)

These are the sources where I have personally verified that textbook PDFs can be downloaded without registration, as of 2026-02-21. Organized by reliability.

### Internet Archive — Arabic School Textbooks

The single best source. All collections below are freely downloadable in PDF format, hosted permanently on archive.org. Originally uploaded by the [al3arabiya.org](https://al3arabiya.org/) project.

#### Iraq — 33 textbooks, grades 1-12
- **URL**: https://archive.org/details/ArabicIraqTextBooks
- **Status**: VERIFIED — free download, CC0 license
- **Contents**: Primary (Y1-Y6), middle (MY1-MY3), high school (H4-H6). Covers reading, grammar, and literature. 2.5 GB total.
- **File naming**: `Arabic_Iraq_I_Y1` (primary year 1), `Arabic_Iraq_I_MY1` (middle year 1), `Arabic_Iraq_H4` (high school year 4)
- **Direct example**: https://archive.org/download/ArabicIraqTextBooks/Arabic_Iraq_I_Y1.pdf (Grade 1 reading)

#### Kuwait — 62 textbooks, grades 1-12 (full set)
- **URL**: https://archive.org/details/KuwaitTextBooksFullSetYear1-12
- **Status**: VERIFIED — free download, 983 MB total
- **Contents**: Complete Arabic language curriculum for all 12 grades. Includes student books and workbooks (karasah). Multiple volumes per grade for upper levels.
- **Also available as ZIP**: https://archive.org/details/ArabicStudentsBooksKuwait (220 MB compressed)

#### Syria — 24 textbooks, grades 1-10
- **URL**: https://archive.org/details/SyriaArabicTextBooks
- **Status**: VERIFIED — free download, 2.2 GB total
- **Contents**: Grades 1-10 with textbooks (TB), student books (SB), and student notes (SN) variations.
- **Source**: al3arabiya.org, uploaded 2013

#### Palestine — 40 textbooks, grades 1-11
- **URL**: https://archive.org/details/Arabic_textbook_Palestine_356
- **Status**: VERIFIED — free download
- **Contents**: "لغتنا الجميلة" (Our Beautiful Language) grades 1-7 (2 parts each), linguistic sciences grades 8-9, literature grades 10-11, plus calligraphy (grades 1-6) and Quran/tajweed (grades 5-10). Includes solution keys.
- **Also**: https://archive.org/details/Arabic_textbook_Palestine (additional collection)

#### Saudi Arabia — 31 high school textbooks
- **URL**: https://archive.org/details/SaudiHighSchoolArabicTextBooks
- **Status**: VERIFIED — free download
- **Contents**: Grades 10-12. Arabic literature (Adab), grammar (Gram), religious studies, reading comprehension, Islamic history, Quran recitation (Qirat), jurisprudence (Fiqh).
- **File naming**: `SaudiHS_Y10_Adab`, `SaudiHS_Y11_Gram`, etc.
- **Also old middle school**: https://archive.org/details/SaudiOldMidSchoolTextBooksArabic
- **Also 2012-2013 elementary**: https://archive.org/details/abdulrazzaqshariff_gmail — "لغتي" (Lughati) for grades 1-4

#### Egypt — 5th and 6th primary
- **URL**: https://archive.org/details/Arabic-School-Books-5th-primary-1st-2nd-term-Khawagah-2019-
- **Status**: VERIFIED — free download, 2019 editions
- **Contents**: 5th primary Arabic, both semesters. 6th primary also available (search "Arabic-School-Books-6th-primary" on archive.org).

#### Other Archive.org Collections
- **Arabic books by language**: https://archive.org/details/booksbylanguage_arabic — massive collection including school textbooks, classical literature, religious texts
- **Arabic schoolbooks general**: https://archive.org/details/20201009_20201009_2009
- **Saudi old textbooks**: https://archive.org/details/SaudiOldTextBook

### Jordan — schoolbooks.jo1jo.com (Direct PDF Links)

- **URL**: https://schoolbooks.jo1jo.com/
- **Status**: VERIFIED — direct PDF downloads, no registration
- **Grades**: 1-12, all subjects, both semesters, plus teacher guides
- **Current edition**: 2024/2025 curriculum
- **URL pattern**: `https://schoolbooks.jo1jo.com/{grade_number}.htm`
- **Grade 1 example**: https://schoolbooks.jo1jo.com/1.htm
- **Arabic language books for Grade 1**:
  - Student Book Part 1 (2024)
  - Student Book Part 2 (2025)
  - Exercises Parts 1 & 2
  - Writing Workbook
- **All subjects available**: Arabic, math, science, English, Islamic education, social studies, PE, art

### Lebanon — CRDP Official PDFs

- **URL**: https://www.crdp.org/books-pdf
- **Status**: VERIFIED — direct PDF downloads from official government site
- **Grades**: Kindergarten (3 levels) through secondary year 3 (with branch specializations)
- **How it works**: Each textbook has a "تحميل الملف" (download file) link pointing to `/sites/default/files/` on the CRDP server
- **Digital textbooks also at**: https://www.crdp.org/en_test_crd/books-digital

### Morocco — Google Drive Links via taalime.com

- **URL**: https://www.taalime.com/2023/07/pdf.html
- **Status**: VERIFIED — Google Drive download links
- **Grade 1 Arabic textbooks**:
  - "المفيد في اللغة العربية" (Al-Mufid): https://drive.google.com/file/d/14tsm9PvP8M4ZRhIwbikv2q6sq2F7kbM0/view
  - "كتابي في اللغة العربية" (Kitabi): https://drive.google.com/file/d/1OuzqMPSisLQyBo5lufEvYFdMLIE7rNqF/view
- **Note**: Morocco uses multiple textbook publishers (not a single national textbook), so several options per grade.

### Libya — Google Drive Links via libyanschoolkl.com

- **URL**: https://www.libyanschoolkl.com/الكتاب-المدرسي
- **Status**: VERIFIED — Google Drive folder distribution
- **Grades**: 1-12
- **Source**: Center for Educational Curricula and Educational Research, Ministry of Education

## 2. Government Ministry Portals

Official sources that require navigation or accounts but provide current-edition textbooks.

### Saudi Arabia — IEN Portal
- **URL**: https://ien.edu.sa/ (books: https://ienbooks.t4edu.com/)
- **Grades**: K-12, all subjects, kindergarten, adult literacy, special education
- **Format**: E-reader interface (no direct PDF download)
- **Access**: Free to browse; works through their e-reader, not as raw PDFs
- **Arabic textbook name**: "لغتي" (Lughati) / "لغتي الجميلة" (Lughati al-Jamila)
- **Third-party PDF mirrors**: mnhaji.com, wajibati.net (view + download of Saudi curriculum)

### Egypt — Ministry Electronic Library
- **URL**: https://ellibrary.moe.gov.eg/books/
- **Also**: https://studentbooks.moe.gov.eg/
- **Grades**: KG through secondary, all subjects, 2025-2026 edition
- **Format**: Dynamic dropdown selector — pick stage → grade → semester → subject → book type
- **Access**: Free, no registration required
- **No static URLs** — you must navigate the dropdown interface

### Egypt — Education 2.0 (Reformed Curriculum)
- **URL**: https://edu2-egypt.com/
- **Grades**: KG through Grade 6 (reformed curriculum only, 2018+)
- **Format**: PDF
- **Access**: Free, Creative Commons (Share, Adapt, Attribution, ShareAlike)
- **Note**: Only covers the newer reformed curriculum, not grades 7-12

### Jordan — Ministry of Education
- **URL**: https://moe.gov.jo/ar/node/58 (curriculum administration)
- **URL**: https://moe.gov.jo/ar/node/84606 (National Curriculum Development Center editions)
- **Grades**: 1-12, all subjects
- **Format**: PDF
- **Access**: Free
- **Better option**: Use schoolbooks.jo1jo.com (see section 1) for direct PDF links

### UAE — Diwan eBook Reader
- **URL**: https://www.moe.gov.ae/En/pages/ereader.aspx
- **Grades**: All
- **Format**: App-based (Diwan app for iOS/Android)
- **Access**: Requires government school student/teacher account
- **No open PDF downloads available**

### Iraq — General Directorate of Curricula
- **URL**: https://manahj.edu.iq/
- **Status**: Returns 403 — may be restricted or intermittently available
- **Third-party mirrors** (unverified):
  - https://iraqiteacher.com/books
  - https://iqeducational.com/iraqi-curriculum-books/
- **Best option**: Use Internet Archive collection (see section 1)

### Palestine — Rawafed Educational Portal
- **URL**: https://new.rawafed.edu.ps/ar/grade-books
- **Grades**: Basic stage (1-10), grades 11-12 with specializations
- **Access**: 222 textbooks available, dynamically loaded via JavaScript
- **No static PDF URLs** — requires interacting with the page
- **Best option**: Use Internet Archive collection (see section 1)

### Qatar — Ministry of Education
- **URL**: https://www.edu.gov.qa/en/
- **No open PDF portal found**
- **Qatar National Library**: offers "I Read Arabic" digital reading program
- **Best option**: Use almanahj.com Qatar section (https://almanahj.com/qa/)

### Kuwait — Government e-Books
- **URL**: https://e.gov.kw/sites/kgoenglish/Pages/eServices/MOE/EBooksLibrary.aspx
- **Best option**: Use Internet Archive collections (see section 1 — 62 textbooks verified)

### Bahrain — Ministry of Education
- **URL**: https://www.moe.gov.bh/archive/books.aspx?lan=ar
- **Status**: Returns 403 — may be deprecated
- **Educational resources**: https://moe.gov.bh/en/moe-educational-resources

### Oman — Ministry of Education
- **URL**: https://home.moe.gov.om/
- **Format**: Available via Ministry LMS
- **Access**: Likely requires student/teacher credentials

## 3. Multi-Country Aggregator Sites

### al3arabiya.org — VERIFIED
- **URL**: https://al3arabiya.org/الكتب-المدرسية-text-books/
- **Countries**: Egypt, Palestine, Kuwait, Syria, Algeria, Iraq, Saudi Arabia
- **How it works**: Each country links to an Internet Archive collection with freely downloadable PDFs
- **Access**: Completely free, no registration
- **The most important aggregator** — all materials permanently hosted on Internet Archive

### almanahj.com — PARTIALLY VERIFIED
- **URL**: https://almanahj.com/
- **Countries**: UAE, Oman, Saudi Arabia, Bahrain, Qatar, Kuwait, Egypt
- **URL patterns**:
  - Country page: `https://almanahj.com/{country_code}/`
  - Grade page: `https://almanahj.com/{country_code}/grade{N}`
  - Subject filter: `https://almanahj.com/{country_code}/{grade}arabic{semester}/book`
  - Individual file: `https://almanahj.com/{country_code}/id={number}`
- **Country codes**: sa (Saudi), eg (Egypt), qa (Qatar), ae (UAE), om (Oman), bh (Bahrain), kw (Kuwait)
- **Note**: Some Saudi textbooks removed due to IP complaints (redirect to moe.gov.sa). Other countries may still work.
- **Access**: Free, no registration, also has mobile app

## 5. Digital Libraries and Book Collections

### Arabic Collections Online (ACO) — NYU
- **URL**: https://dlib.nyu.edu/aco/ | Browse: https://dlib.nyu.edu/aco/browse/
- **Contents**: 17,699 volumes across 10,473 subjects. Public domain Arabic-language content from NYU, Princeton, Cornell, Columbia, AUC, AUB, UAE National Archives.
- **Access**: Completely free, open access

### Hindawi Foundation
- **URL**: https://www.hindawi.org/
- **Contents**: 1,745 books (81.5 million words). Non-fiction, novels, children's literature, poetry, plays.
- **Format**: HTML and plain text (clean digital text, not scans)
- **Access**: Completely free

### Al-Maktabah al-Shamela
- **URL**: https://shamela.ws/
- **Internet Archive bulk**: https://archive.org/details/Shamila-15000books (15,000 books)
- **Contents**: 15,000+ classical Islamic and Arabic texts. Quran exegesis, hadith, jurisprudence, grammar, literature, history.
- **Format**: Desktop app + web search + bulk download
- **Access**: Free

### Al-Maktabah al-Waqfiyya
- **URL**: https://waqfeya.net/
- **Contents**: 1,000+ books on Islamic studies, Arabic language, literature, history. Also English reference works (Lane's lexicon, Hans Wehr, Wright's grammar).
- **Format**: ZIP/RAR containing PDF
- **Access**: Free

### Noor Library
- **URL**: https://www.noor-book.com/en/
- **Contents**: Thousands of Arabic e-books across all fields
- **Access**: Free

## Notes for Alif

For MSA reading comprehension, the most valuable verified sources are:

1. **Internet Archive collections** — 190+ freely downloadable Arabic textbooks from 6 countries. Primary school Arabic textbooks use simple, graded MSA with natural difficulty progression. Already in PDF, easy to bulk-download via `ia` CLI tool.

2. **Jordan schoolbooks.jo1jo.com** — Complete current (2024/2025) Jordanian curriculum with direct PDF links. Jordan is known for high-quality Arabic language textbooks.

3. **Lebanon CRDP** — Official government PDFs for all grades. Lebanese curriculum known for strong Arabic language arts.

4. **al3arabiya.org** — Master index linking to all Internet Archive collections. Best starting point for multi-country comparison.

5. **Arabic Collections Online (NYU)** — 17,699 public domain volumes. Rich source of authentic MSA reading across many genres.

6. **Hindawi Foundation** — 1,745 modern Arabic books in clean digital text. Ideal for text extraction.
