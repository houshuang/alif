V11: Mini Teaching Moments
Target A. After each confusable, a one-line teaching note explains the letter difference.
👁 EASILY CONFUSED
نَظَرَ
naẓara — to look
نَطَرَ to guard dots only
Same shape — ظ (ẓā) has a dot above, ط (ṭā) doesn’t
نَشَرَ to publish
ظ (ẓā) is tall with dot · ش (shīn) has 3 dots and teeth
نَصَرَ to help
ظ (ẓā) is open/tall · ص (ṣād) is closed/round
بَصَرَ to see
Two letters differ: ن↔ب (dot above vs below) and ظ↔ص (open vs closed)
