Listen to each voice, rate them. Focus on: emphatic consonant clarity, naturalness, learner-friendliness.

What to listen for

Emphatics test: Does the voice distinguish between نَظَرَ (nazara, with ظ) and ضَرَبَ (daraba, with ض)?
Story test: Does it sound natural? Good pacing? Clear pronunciation of diacritics?
